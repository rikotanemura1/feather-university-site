How to Make a Peanut Butter Sandwich

  1. Gather your ingredients: peanut butter, two slices of bread, a knife or spreader, and optional jelly.
  2. Lay out the bread: Place the two slices of bread on a clean surface.
  3. Spread the peanut butter: Use the knife to spread an even layer of peanut butter on one side of one slice.
  4. Add jelly (optional): If you like, spread jelly on one side of the other slice.
  5. Assemble the sandwich: Put the two slices together with the spreads facing each other.
  6. Serve and enjoy!
